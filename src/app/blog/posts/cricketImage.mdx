## Introduction

After building tools like PagePulse and FocusGuard, I wanted to build something bigger.

Not just a tool…  
but a complete system.

Something that includes:
- users  
- data  
- payments  
- real-world use case  

That’s how the idea of **CricketImages** came.

---

## The Idea

Cricket is one of the most followed sports.

But finding high-quality images is not easy.

Most platforms:
- don’t have proper categories  
- don’t provide premium quality  
- don’t have structured access  

So I decided to build a platform where users can:

- explore cricket images  
- download free content  
- buy premium images  
- use subscriptions  

---

## What I Am Building

CricketImages is a **full-stack platform**.

It includes:

- frontend (UI + UX)  
- backend (API + logic)  
- database (data storage)  
- authentication  
- payment system  

---

## Core Features

### 1. Image Discovery

Users can explore images based on:

- players  
- matches  
- categories  
- highlights  

The goal is to make searching simple and fast.

---

### 2. Free & Premium System

There are two types of content:

- free images → accessible to all  
- premium images → require purchase or subscription  

This creates a monetization system.

---

### 3. Authentication System

Users can:

- sign up  
- log in  
- manage their account  

This is important for:

- purchases  
- downloads  
- personalization  

---

### 4. Cart & Checkout

Users can:

- add images to cart  
- purchase multiple images  
- complete payment  

This makes it a real product, not just a gallery.

---

### 5. Subscription Model

Instead of buying every image:

- users can subscribe  
- get access to premium content  

This improves user experience and revenue model.

---

## Tech Stack

### Frontend

### Backend
- Node.js  
- Express

- MongoDB  

### Storage

### Authentication

### Payments
- Stripe / Razorpay  

---

## Challenges (Planned & Ongoing)

Since this is a full system, challenges are bigger.

### 1. Data structure

Managing:

- images  
- users  
- purchases  
- subscriptions  

requires a proper schema design.

---

### 2. Secure content

Premium images must be:

- protected  
- not publicly accessible  

This requires controlled delivery.

---

### 3. Payment flow

Handling:

- success / failure  
- order tracking  
- user access  

needs careful backend logic.
